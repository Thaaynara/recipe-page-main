Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2–3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well mixed. Add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture (optional).
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to coat the surface evenly.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but remain slightly runny in the middle, add fillings over one half.
  5. Fold and serve: Carefully lift one edge and fold the omelette over the fillings. Cook for another minute then slide onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot with extra salt and pepper if needed.

Nutrition (per serving)

  • Calories: 277 kcal
  • Carbs: 0 g
  • Protein: 20 g
  • Fat: 22 g
